Research

My research interests broadly concern visualization-empowered AI systems, a direction I began to develop after starting my PhD. I hope to build visual analytics systems that integrate AI techniques with human intelligence, so that AI can use humans' input as a guardrail and produce understandable, acceptable output for humans in return.
More recently, my research directions have focused on tabular synthetic data and chart understanding using VLMs.
